Youth Justice

I undertake psychiatric reports for the following types of cases:

  1. Psychiatric Reports and assessments in criminal cases for Crown, Youth and Magistrates' Courts.
  2. Assessment for ADHD (Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder), particularly for repeat offenders.
  3. Assessment for Learning Disability in the context of offending. This may include, amongst others, those with Autism Spectrum Disorder.
  4. Psychiatric Assessments for Fitness to Plead cases
  5. Pre-sentencing Assessments
  6. Assessment of Dangerousness / Risk Assessment
  7. Sexual Crimes including sexual harassment
  8. Arson
  9. Violent Criminal Assessments including Murder
  10. Violence /Self Harm/Suicide
  11. Risk assessment for planning release from custody.
  12. Stalking
  13. Abnormal Behaviour
