About Me

About Me

Dr Girish Vaidya

MBBS, MD (Psychological Medicine), MRCPsych
Consultant Child and Adolescent Psychiatrist

I have over 20 years' experience of psychiatry across two continents. I have particular experience and expertise in mental health problems in children and young adults.

I accept instructions from:

  1. Solicitors acting on behalf of young offenders
  2. Local Authorities looking for advice on placements
  3. Single joint expert in Care Proceedings

I have also provided second / third opinions on complex cases to NHS Trusts.

I am CRB checked and am an Approved Clinician under the Mental Health Act 2007. I work in the NHS in a Forensic Child and Adolescent Mental Health Service (CAMHS). I also provide psychiatric input to the mental health team for Looked After Children (LAC) in Sheffield.

I am a member of the Society of Expert Witnesses and UK Register of Expert Witnesses. I adhere to LSC costs schedule in all legal cases. Non-legal cases carry fixed costs. I have Professional Indemnity for my medico-legal work.
